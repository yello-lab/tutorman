Passing Core Mathematics

  • Instructor-led Classroom | Virtual

Overview

Course Description

The "Passing Core Maths" course is designed to equip students with the essential mathematical skills and knowledge needed to succeed in their studies and everyday life. This course focuses on key mathematical concepts and problem-solving techniques that are vital for academic achievement and practical applications.

Through a combination of theoretical understanding and hands-on practice, students will build confidence in various areas of mathematics, preparing them for examinations and further studies in related subjects.

What you'll learn

  • Introduction to Core Maths and Its Importance

  • Numbers and Number Systems

  • Algebraic Concepts and Techniques

  • Functions and Graphs

  • Statistics and Data Analysis

  • Probability and Its Applications

  • Measurement and Geometry

  • Problem-Solving Strategies

  • Preparing for Core Maths Assessments

Who should attend

  • High School Students

  • Students preparing for Core Maths examinations

  • Adults returning to education

  • Individuals seeking to improve their mathematical skills

  • Students in vocational or technical programs requiring maths

Requirements

  • No prior knowledge of advanced mathematics is required.

  • Access to a computer or calculator for practice and assessments.

  • A willingness to learn and engage with mathematical concepts.
